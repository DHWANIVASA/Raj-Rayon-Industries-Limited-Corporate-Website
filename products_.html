Product Range

Our Products

A complete range of polyester yarns designed for various textile applications worldwide.

Polyester Textured Yarn (PTY)

Polyester Textured Yarn (PTY)

Crimped polyester yarn with excellent bulk, elasticity, and comfort — ideal for hosiery and knitwear.

Specifications

  • Denier Range: 20D to 300D
  • Tenacity: 4.5–5.5 g/d
  • Elongation: 20–25%
  • Shrinkage: < 3%

Applications

  • ✓ Hosiery
  • ✓ Knitwear
  • ✓ Upholstery
  • ✓ Sports Apparel
Partially Oriented Yarn (POY)

Partially Oriented Yarn (POY)

Semi-crystalline yarn used as feed for texturing and false twist operations. Superior processing characteristics.

Specifications

  • Denier Range: 50D to 450D
  • Tenacity: 1.2–1.5 g/d
  • Birefringence: 0.055–0.065
  • Melt Index: Consistent

Applications

  • ✓ Texturing Feed
  • ✓ False Twist
  • ✓ Industrial Fabrics
  • ✓ Carpet Yarn
Fully Drawn Yarn (FDY)

Fully Drawn Yarn (FDY)

High-tenacity yarn ready for direct use in weaving and knitting. Excellent dyeability and performance.

Specifications

  • Denier Range: 30D to 300D
  • Tenacity: 4.2–5.0 g/d
  • Elongation: 15–20%
  • Luster: Semi/Dull

Applications

  • ✓ Woven Fabrics
  • ✓ Knitted Wear
  • ✓ Premium Textiles
  • ✓ Technical Textiles
Doped Dyed Yarn (DDY)

Doped Dyed Yarn (DDY)

Solution dyed polyester yarn produced by adding pigments during the polymer stage, providing excellent colour fastness, durability, and uniform shade throughout the fibre.

Specifications

  • Denier Range: 20D to 300D
  • Tenacity: 4.0–6.0 g/d
  • Elongation: 20–30%
  • Shrinkage: < 3%

Applications

  • ✓ Sportswear
  • ✓ Activewear
  • ✓ Hosiery
  • ✓ Home Textiles
  • ✓ Upholstery
