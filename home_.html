About Us

Weaving Excellence
Since Three Decades

Raj Rayon Industries Limited (Formerly known as Raj Rayon Ltd.), a Public Ltd Company, listed on the Bombay Stock Exchange & National Stock Exchange, is mainly engaged in manufacturing of Polyester Texturised Yarn (PTY), Partially Oriented Yarn (POY), & Fully Drawn Yarn (FDY).

Our plants are located at Silvassa, in the Union Territory of Dadra & Nagar Haveli, India. We pride ourselves on delivering world-class polyester yarn that powers textile manufacturing across India and beyond.
